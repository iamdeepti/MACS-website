About us


"Mathematics is to Computer Science what notes are to music. Go down deep enough into anything and you will find mathematics ." MACS (Mathematics and Computing Society), the student body of the Applied Mathematics Department of DTU was established in 2013 and since then it aims to provide a platform to its students to showcase, sharpen and hone their talents through various technical and non technical competitions. Also the society focuses upon learning through workshops, SIGs, seminars, guest lectures and various symposiums.
