INVICTUS

INVICTUS is the yearly fest organized by DTU as an attempt to help students showcase their immense technical and informal skills .

INVICTUS incorporates within itself a plentitude of technical events like programming contests, website designing, and quizzes. It also has an ever widening array of technical and informal events such as Sudoku, Anti - Chess and LAN - Gaming. Bringing a respite from the hard curriculum of engineering, fests have always been a very integral part of college life. Fests offer vast technical opportunities alongside being a medium for students to bond together socially. INVICTUS is a platform for colleges from all across the country to showcase their talents.
